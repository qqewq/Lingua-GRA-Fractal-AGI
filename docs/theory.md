## üá¨üáß 7. English summary (short)

This section gives a concise English summary of the core theoretical ideas behind Lingua GRA.

### 7.1. Multi-level GRA

We model the language system as a state \(|\Psi\rangle\) in a tensor product of Hilbert spaces:

\[
|\Psi\rangle \in \mathcal{H}_{\text{lang}}
= \bigotimes_{l=0}^{L} \mathcal{H}^{(l)},
\]

with each level \(l\) having a goal subspace \(\mathcal{S}_l\) and a projector \(\mathcal{P}_{G_l}\). The **foam** (penalty) at level \(l\) is

\[
\Phi^{(l)}(\Psi^{(l)}, G_l) = \|(1 - \mathcal{P}_{G_l}) \Psi^{(l)}\|^2,
\]

and the global GRA functional

\[
J_{\text{GRA}}(\Psi) = \sum_{l=0}^{L} \Lambda_l \, \Phi^{(l)}(\Psi^{(l)}, G_l).
\]

Minimizing \(J_{\text{GRA}}\) yields states that are consistent with all goals across all levels.

### 7.2. Multiverse formulation

A multiverse index \(\mathbf{a} = (a_0,\dots,a_k)\) allows us to organize multiple agents, domains, and meta-systems into a single Hilbert space \(\mathcal{H}_{\text{multiverse}}\). A recursive multiverse functional \(J_{\text{multiverse}}\) aggregates local foams and higher-level consistency terms. Under commutativity and hierarchical consistency of projectors, a **full multiverse annihilation** state exists with zero foam across all levels.

### 7.3. Language levels

Lingua GRA interprets levels as:

- symbolic (tokens),
- syntactic (parse trees),
- semantic (embeddings, knowledge graphs),
- pragmatic (agent-in-environment, goals),
- meta-linguistic (language about language and architectures).

The language of an AGI is thus a multi-level GRA system defined over these spaces.

### 7.4. Fractal geometry

Recent work shows that:

- word2vec embeddings of many languages form multifractal structures with distinct fractal dimensions at short and long scales;[web:38][web:34]  
- intrinsic dimensions of language fractal structures (embeddings of n-grams) are non-integer, around 9 for Russian and English;[web:45]  
- correlation dimension of natural language measured via Grassberger‚ÄìProcaccia in a statistical manifold yields a universal value around 6.5.[web:105][web:41][web:44]

In Lingua GRA, these observations motivate a **fractal regularizer**:

\[
R_{\text{fract}}(\Psi^{(l)}) =
\Big(D^{(l)}_{\text{current}} - D^{(l)}_{\text{target}}\Big)^2,
\]

added to each level‚Äôs foam to enforce a desired fractal profile of the representation space.

### 7.5. AGI self-improvement

AGI agents minimize a combined functional \(J_{\text{fractal}}\), improving both:

- logical/linguistic consistency (GRA annihilation),
- geometric structure of their internal language (fractal optimality).

Meta-levels can adapt projectors and target fractal dimensions based on successful scientific communication and problem solving, making the language itself a vehicle for **self-referential self-improvement**.
